Hiya! I'm Monica.

I'm a Senior Engineer at Google, where I work on Magenta, making music and art with Machine Learning. Before this I worked on Polymer, and was a big advocate for Web Components. Before that, I used to work on Chrome. Do you know that little button that has appeared in top right corner of your browser? Yeah, I did that (but now it looks different. It's been 6 years after all).

I have a GitHub AMA repo where you can ask me things, because I heard that's what the cool kids do nowadays. Or you can send me an email. Or both.

Because the internet is a big place, standard disclaimers about these being my views and not my employer's apply.

Monica works on Magenta, in Google Brain, where she makes generative music and art with Machine Learning. In the past she's worked on Polymer, Web Components, and Chrome, and has probably at least once broken the Internet for you.
